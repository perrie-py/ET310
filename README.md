# PP Attachment Ambiguity in ChatGPT

An empirical study investigating whether ChatGPT reliably detects prepositional phrase (PP) attachment ambiguity, and whether pragmatic plausibility affects its response. 

## Research Questions
- Does ChatGPT consistently identify syntactic ambiguity in PP constructions?
- Does pragmatic plausibility (regular vs. inverse sentences) affect ChatGPT's attachment preferences?

## Method
- 300 trials accross 30 constructed sentences (instrumental, locative, descriptive PPs)
- Controlled elicitation design with standardised prompting
- Responses coded and analysed using cho-squared tests in R

## Key Findings 
- ChatGPT detected syntactic ambiguity in 83% of cases overall
- Detection rates varied significantly by PP type (p = 0.018)
- Pragmatic plausibility was a significant predictor in regular conditions but not inverse conditions
